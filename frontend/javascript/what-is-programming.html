What is programming

Programming in general is instructions for the computer to do something: tell me to rest, wait 10 minutes and then do it again until you have done it 6 times. If someone presses this button, take their message they have written and send it to me. then clean the message area

computers operate on electric signals that are either on or off. By combining these with logic, they can do complex things.

instructions to a computer must eventually be these on or off messages. so 1 and 0.

A human could never understand them, so that is why we have invented programming languages to help us write the commands.

the languages are translated on the fly (interpreted) or translated before anything happens (compiled) from a language that's easier for us to understand to a form that a machine can understand: machine code that is teh ones and zeroes that represent electric pulses in the computers processor.
